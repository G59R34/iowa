Our Story

QuantumHeat began in 2018 when our founder, Dr. Sarah Chen, made a groundbreaking discovery in quantum field manipulation while working on her PhD in theoretical physics. She realized that quantum principles could be applied to microwave technology to create unprecedented heating precision and energy efficiency.

What started as a research project in a Stanford University lab has evolved into a revolutionary company that's redefining kitchen technology. Our patented QuantumField‚Ñ¢ technology allows for molecular-level heating control, reducing cooking times by 70% while preserving nutrients and flavors.

Today, we're proud to be at the forefront of smart kitchen innovation, with our products used by professional chefs and home cooks in over 40 countries worldwide.
